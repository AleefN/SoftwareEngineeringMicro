ABOUT PAGE

I developed this project for my Software Engineering Microcredential course. My project idea stemmed from the thought that greetings are important. Through it, people call upon each other, whether it be in a informal or formal manner. In other words, greetings compose the basic element of speech, language, and communication. So, I created a table outlining how to say hello in other languages. I hope you find this website helpful, cool, or interesting. Love, Aleef!
